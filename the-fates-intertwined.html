    Feels the minecraft weapons progression is very boring? Or the boss experience that feels very plains? try this add-on, this add-on adds a lot of weapons progression, some bosses, and even added the custom lore based on the 4 craftable weapons, and defeat all the boss to get more weapons. many of the custom weapons here have their unique attack pattern, and they have the special ability when you interact with this weapon

    The Fates Intertwined is an Add-ons for minecraft that focus on the combat and battle style, having 9 unique legendary weapon that have attack animation and have their own unique abilities like dashing and other abiities and adding some useful items making the legendary combat easier, there are 60+ content in this addon including :

    - 9 Unique Weapons
    - 49 Custom Items (Weapons included)
    - 3 New Bosses
    - 1 New Structure
    - 3 New Blocks
    - and Over 50 Recipes

    Weapons :

    Winterbloom Sword :

    A sword with two style, Keris and Sword fused into one and makes the truly legendary one. this sword has main ability of teleport-dashing, kind of dash, but instant tp that place, and this sword has 4 attack pattern that represent the magic ability, this kind of sword have power to obliterate the boss more quickly. Winterbloom Sword have 3 Skill ability :

    Lee-Enfield : Break :

    When cast this ability, will create the forcefield that damage your surroundings and give them break effect that slows their movement, this ability is great because the radius is about 10 blocks and 10 seconds of the skill duration. But this skill have slightly long cooldown, 40 Seconds of cooldown will balance the skill effect

    I AM THE HUNTER :

    This skill will teleports you to the nearest target, the target list are players and boss. this skill will deal a lot of damage to Boss, but weaker damage at player. This skill prioritize the boss first then player because how effective the skill is to boss. Besides of that, this skill has very long of cooldown, 140 Seconds when the target is found, 60 Seconds when no target has found

    Lee-Enfield : Absolute Zero :

    This is the ultimate, when casting this skill, the user will create unstoppable force that will summon the lasers that have the absolute coldness. Any entities that near the ultimate will suffers some damage before blowing the last Last Laser Strikes the last strikes will damage you a lot, especially a boss, except this ultimate have 150 Seconds of cooldown

    Rage of Sakura :

    A katana with the sakura and blood aura that surrounds the sword with serrated shape. This sword have the ability of manipulating the user health to make good damage, this sword have 5 Attack Pattern and 3 Skills that have connection of your health

    Love is Love :

    When casting this skill, the user will heal themself for 8 HP and adds the maximum caps of their health to 40 HP but lasts for a short time. this buff have 60 seconds of duration. this skill maybe not too balanced because they have 50 seconds of cooldown.

    Questioning Lust :

    When casting this skill, the users will create an area that follows their user, anyone who inside the area, they will get slow and hunger debuff for periods of time. the area have 10 blocks of size and 10 seconds of duration. What makes this skill balanced is the cooldown only have 30 Seconds

    Blinding Heartbroken :

    The ultimate will make around the user blind and start withering. the user will suffers the withering effects too, but slightly slower and have speed and jump height increase. this debuff lasts over 15 seconds, and the cooldown of this ultimate is 120 seconds
